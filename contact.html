Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum qualification required?
Students must have passed their 12th standard (10+2) with Physics and Math to apply for pilot training.
What is the total cost of CPL training in Kazakhstan?
The total cost is significantly lower than USA or Australia — save 30-40% compared to Western academies. Contact us for a detailed fee breakdown.
Do you help with DGCA license conversion?
Yes! Our experts guide you through the entire DGCA conversion process, including exam preparation and mandatory flying checks in Indian airspace.
How long does the CPL training take?
The CPL program typically takes 14-18 months, including ground school and the required 200 flying hours.
