About CIS Aviation: Your Co-Pilot in Global Education

CIS Aviation is the newest vertical of the CIS Group, a name synonymous with trust and international education consultancy. Following the success of CIS MBBS, where we have guided thousands of students toward medical excellence, we are now taking flight to support the next generation of Commercial Pilots.

We understand that the path to the cockpit is paved with complex regulations, rigorous training, and significant investment. That's why we've streamlined the process, specializing in:

  • Elite Training in Kazakhstan: Partner exclusively with top-tier flying schools offering state-of-the-art glass cockpit aircraft.
  • Seamless DGCA Conversion: Experts guide you through the DGCA license conversion seamlessly.
  • End-to-End Mentorship: From admissions to post-training licensing, we are with you from takeoff to touchdown.

Our Mission

To make world-class pilot training accessible, transparent, and result-oriented for every aspiring aviator in India.
