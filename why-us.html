Our Process

The CIS Aviation Flight Plan: 5 Steps to Your Wings

Navigating your pilot career shouldn't feel like flying through a storm. Here is our streamlined process:

Step 1: Pre-Flight Consultation & Eligibility

Before you pack your bags, we ensure you are ready for takeoff. We review your 10+2 educational background (Physics and Math) and guide you through the Class II Medical Examination, a mandatory requirement before starting your journey.

Step 2: Admission to Kazakhstan's Top Flight Schools

We handle the heavy lifting of the application process. We facilitate your enrollment in Kazakhstan's premier aviation academies, known for their modern fleets and international training standards. We manage transcripts, invitation letters, and visa processing.

Step 3: On-Ground Training (CPL Phase)

Once in Kazakhstan, you begin your journey. This includes Ground School covering Air Navigation, Meteorology, and Aircraft Technical, plus completing the required 200 hours of flying on modern glass-cockpit aircraft.

Step 4: The DGCA Conversion Bridge

While training in Kazakhstan, our Indian team prepares your paperwork. Upon your return, we guide you through the DGCA requirements — clearing conversion exams and completing mandatory flying checks in Indian airspace.

Step 5: Command the Skies

With your DGCA-stamped CPL in hand, you are ready for airline recruitment. We provide guidance on Type Rating and airline-specific entrance preparations to land your first job as a First Officer.

Why Choose Kazakhstan?

The "Hidden Gem" of aviation training — world-class standards at unbeatable affordability.

Massive Cost Advantage

Save 30-40% compared to Western academies. Affordable tuition and low living expenses so you can focus on your flight hours.

Modern Glass-Cockpit Fleet

Train on Diamond DA40/DA42 or Cessna 172 equipped with Garmin G1000 NXi avionics — the same technology used by commercial airlines.

ICAO & DGCA Compliance

Kazakhstan's standards are strictly aligned with ICAO. CIS Aviation selects schools whose training is recognized for smooth DGCA conversion.

Optimal Training Environment

Diverse weather conditions build superior piloting skills. Open, uncongested airspace means no long waiting times — you fly more.

Proximity to India

Just a 4-5 hour flight from major Indian cities. Stay connected with home and reduce travel costs for parents during visits.

International Exposure

Fly with experienced international instructors, getting globally recognized training that sets you apart in job interviews.
